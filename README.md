# Sales & Business Performance Analysis — Tableau Dashboard

**Author:** Sayali Lohokare
**Tool:** Tableau (Tableau Public / Desktop)

## What this project is about

A multi-dashboard Tableau project analysing retail sales, profit, and customer behaviour across
regions, product categories, and customer segments, built from a retail sales dataset structured
similarly to Tableau's Sample Superstore dataset (exact source dataset not confirmed), with sales
data spanning Indian states and cities.

The project includes several individual dashboards plus two presentation-style Tableau Stories that
walk through the analysis narrative end to end, from overall business performance down to
profitability drivers.

## Dashboards included

- **Sales Overview** — KPI summary cards (Avg. Discount, Profit, Quantity, Sales), sales by region,
  and a sales trend chart over time.
- **Product Analysis** — profit by sub-category, sales by category, and quantity sold by category.
- **Customer Analysis** — top 10 customers by sales, customer segment breakdown, and a state-level
  geographic sales map.
- **Profit and Discount Analysis** — profit by category, profit trend over time, and a discount vs.
  profit scatter plot.
- **Regional Analysis** — geographic sales map, sales by city, and region-by-profit comparison.
- **Product Performance** — quantity sold by product, profit by sub-category, and sales by
  sub-category.
- **Customers Overview** — order count by segment, top customers, and region-by-profit.

## Tableau Stories

Two Tableau Story presentations are included, walking through the analysis as a guided narrative:

- **Story 1** — Overall business performance, product/category drivers, and customer segments.
- **Story 2** — A five-part narrative: overall business performance → product/profit drivers →
  regional and city-level performance → customer segmentation → discount's effect on profitability.

## Key insights

- South and East regions generate the highest sales, but profit doesn't track sales evenly across
  regions — some regions have high sales but comparatively low profit.
- The Technology category drives the highest revenue and profit, with Phones and Laptops as the
  strongest-performing sub-categories.
- Higher discount levels are associated with reduced profit, with some high-discount orders resulting
  in a loss.
- A small number of cities (e.g. Bengaluru, Bhubaneswar, Ranchi) account for a disproportionate share
  of total sales.
